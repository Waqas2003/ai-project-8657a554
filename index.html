For Every Child

UNICEF works in over 190 countries to save children's lives, to defend their rights, and to help them fulfill their potential.

Our Programs

Education

UNICEF works to ensure that all children, regardless of their background or circumstances, have access to quality education.

Health

UNICEF works to improve the health and well-being of children around the world, focusing on immunization, nutrition, and disease prevention.

Protection

UNICEF works to protect children from violence, exploitation, and abuse, and to ensure that their rights are respected and upheld.
